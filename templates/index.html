Word Equations

Words are represented as points in a high-dimensional space where similar meanings are close together. This lets you do math with words: woman - man gives a vector that represents the relationship that maps man to woman. If that is added to king as woman - man + king = queen the man to woman direction is added to the royalty direction giving a female royalty. Try combining words with + and - to discover semantic relationships.
